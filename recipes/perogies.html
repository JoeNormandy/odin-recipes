Perogies

Description

Grandma's wonderful perogies made from Mrs. T's boxed perogies

Ingredients

  • Mrs. T's Perogies
  • A stick of butter
  • Red Onion

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Put the onions in the butter
  4. Wait until the onions are clear and starting to brown
  5. Add the perogies and cook until brown
